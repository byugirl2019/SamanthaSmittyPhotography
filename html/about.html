Hewwwwooo About Me

Time to meet who's behind the Camera! My name is Samantha Smith, but you can call me Sam! I'm from Idaho but am currently attending college in Utah. I am studying Expereince Design and Management at Brigham Young Univesity. I'm the oldest of 6 girls and love to be outside. You can find me on the volleyball court, climbing and hiking, surfing, and taking pictures throughout it all.

I absolutely LOVE the power of photography. I've had a camera around my neck since I was 12. I live for capturing the real and raw moments. My favorite type of photography is capturing unique cultures from around the world. Some of my favorites have been Cambodia, Italy, and the Philippines.
